How to Play

  • Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors by clicking an icon.
  • Computer will randomly choose its move.
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
  • The one with the higher score after all tries wins.
  • You can replay the game using the "Try Again" button.
