Rock, Paper, Scissors is a classic hand game played between two people. Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand: rock, paper, or scissors. The rules are simple—rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock. The game is often used to make quick decisions or settle disputes in a fun and fair way. It relies on luck, but can also involve strategy and prediction. It's easy to learn and popular worldwide among all ages.
