Instructions for Whac-A-Mole

  • Welcome to Whac-A-Mole! Here's how to play:

  • You earn points by hitting the moles before they disappear.
  • Be quick! The moles don't stay up for long!
  • Try to hit as many moles as possible to get the highest score!
